    Description Ingredients Directions

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a delicious toasty sandwish made with one or more varieties of cheese and bread. Prepare by heating cheese between slices of bread on a griddle until the bread browns and the cheese melts.

Ingredients

  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Sliced havarti
  • Whole lot of butter
  • Sliced sourdough bread

Directions
  1. Heat butter in griddle
  2. Place cheese between sourdough bread
  3. Place in griddle on medium heat
  4. Check frequently until golden-brown, then flip
  5. Cook other side of sandwich
  6. Enjoy!

Grilled cheese originated in the jungles of the San Fernando Valley. Overworked soccer moms needed a quick way to feed an entire troop of soccer players. One mom discovered that she always had cheese and bread in her purse. She decided to throw it on the stove, and added a dollop of butter for good measure; thus, the grilled cheese was born.
